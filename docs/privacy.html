Privacy Policy for semPlanner

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to semPlanner! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage user accounts. If you sign up, we collect basic account info (e.g., email address, display name).
  • Study Materials & Schedules: The syllabi, documents, and timetable images you upload are processed locally or securely transmitted to our backend AI services to generate your study roadmaps and daily schedules.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage data and crash reports to improve the stability of the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the semPlanner service.
  • To generate personalized AI-driven study roadmaps and intelligent task scheduling.
  • To notify you of upcoming classes or study blocks.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, database (Firestore), and hosting.
  • Gemini AI / Google Cloud: Used to power the intelligent parsing and chat features.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. Your study schedules and documents are stored securely via Firebase and are not sold to third parties.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@semplanner.app.

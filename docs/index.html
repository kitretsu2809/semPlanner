Features

  • Intake Anything: Upload photos of your messy timetables, course syllabi, and even your daily meal timings. Our AI understands it all.
  • Goal-Oriented Roadmaps: Tell us your end goal for the semester (e.g., "I want to land a Backend Internship") and our AI will build a personalized, day-by-step roadmap to get you there.
  • Intelligent Scheduling: We automatically find the free gaps between your lectures and meals to schedule high-impact study sessions.
  • Interactive AI Tutor: Chat directly with your uploaded documents to clarify concepts or prepare for midterms instantly.

Why is semPlanner Safe?

We know that providing an API key and uploading your schedule requires trust. Here is how we guarantee your privacy:

  • 🛡️ 100% Local Storage: Your syllabi, timetables, AI chat histories, and personal goals are stored entirely on your device using a local database. We do not have a central database of your documents.
  • 🔑 Direct AI Connection: The app uses your own Gemini API key to communicate directly with Google's servers. We don't route your prompts through any middleman servers, and your API key is only stored securely on your local device, never in the cloud.
  • 🔍 Open Source Transparency: You don't have to take our word for it. semPlanner is completely open source! Developers can inspect our code on GitHub to verify exactly how your data and API key are used.
